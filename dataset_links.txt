Datasets used in this study

1. MS-COCO 2017 validation split
Official source: https://cocodataset.org/

2. LVIS v1.0 validation annotations
Official source: https://www.lvisdataset.org/

Notes:
The datasets are not redistributed in this repository. The main experiments use COCO validation images and annotations. LVIS validation annotations were inspected for rare-category evaluation, but full image-level LVIS evaluation was not reported because only a small number of required images were retrievable in the execution environment.
